Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions we receive from our patients. If you have any other questions, feel free to contact us!

What are your clinic's operating hours?

Our clinic is open Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and on Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 2:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept most major dental insurance plans. Please Contact us to confirm if your insurance is accepted.

How can I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment by using the online booking form on our website ! Book Now

What is your cancellation policy?

If you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment, please give us at least 24 hours' notice to avoid a cancellation fee.

What should I bring to my first appointment?

Please bring your insurance card, a valid ID, and any medical records relevant to your dental health.

How often should I visit the dentist?

We recommend visiting the dentist every six months for regular check-ups and cleanings. However, your dentist may suggest a different schedule based on your individual needs.

What services do you offer?

We offer a wide range of dental services, including cleanings, fillings, root canals, crowns, teeth whitening, and more.

Do you treat children?

Yes, we provide dental care for patients of all ages, including children. We strive to make their visits comfortable and stress-free.

What should I do in case of a dental emergency?

If you experience a dental emergency, please contact us immediately. We will do our best to accommodate you as soon as possible.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans to help make dental care more affordable. Please Contact us for more information.

Can I request a specific dentist?

Yes, you can request a specific dentist when booking your appointment. We will do our best to accommodate your request.
